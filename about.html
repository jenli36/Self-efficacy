Bridge the gap through stairs

Within competitive majors, such as CSE, low self-efficacy is prevalent among students. The stair installations, which can be found in both Allen and Gates centers, show the volume of just how many people struggle with low self-efficacy in hopes to bring awareness to and encourage dialogue about low self-efficacy.
