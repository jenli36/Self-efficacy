Looking for ways to improve your self-efficacy? Check-out these resources! Most of the resources below are cost-free for University of Washington Students!

Counseling Center:

UW Counseling center is a great place to start if you have any questions or concerns about your own self-efficacy. They offer mental health resources, Skills-based workshops, individual and group counseling as well as crisis services.

CSE Advising Counseling:

Paul G Allen school of Computer Science and engineering also have their resources on their advising and counseling page, under the personal counseling section.
