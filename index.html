YOU ARE NOT ALONE.

CSE Self-Efficacy 2019

Within competitive majors, such as CSE, low self-efficacy is prevalent among students. The stair installations, which can be found in both Allen and Gates centers, show the volume just how many people struggle with low self-efficacy in hopes to bring awareness to and encourage dialogue about low self-efficacy.

What is self-efficacy?

Self-efficacy is an individual’s belief in their innate ability to achieve goals. Albert Bandura defines it as a personal judgment of “how well one can execute courses of action required to deal with prospective situations”.

  1. - Easier to withdraw from difficult tasks.
  2. - Have low aspirations and a weaker commitment to the goals.
  3. - Slower to recover from failures.
  4. - Focuses on personal failings and negative outcomes.
  5. - Easier to suffer from stress, isolation, and other mental health problems.
